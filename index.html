Kathryn de la Rosa (she/her) — delarosakathryn@gmail.com

I’m a Pisces and a dramaturg & writer interested in new plays, verse drama, and rural, immigrant and queer narratives. Kentucky born and bred, I’m a 2019-2020 dramaturgy apprentice at Actors Theatre of Louisville.

I earned my BA in theatre & drama and journalism from Indiana University Bloomington. I’ve worked on new plays and researched old ones for The New Harmony Project, Asolo Repertory Theatre, and IU Theatre. My plays been or will be developed in the At First Sight Festival and by University Players.

I’m a media jack-of-all-trades (emphasis on master of none): I stumbled into sound design by way of radio production and coded this website with my bare hands. Journalism training makes me spell it theater.

I’m available for production dramaturgy, script consultation, writing, and anything audio. Send me $5 for a tarot reading.
