Kathryn de la Rosa is a Filipinx playwright, dramaturg and audio producer based in Louisville, working at the intersection of diaspora and provincialism. Their plays span geographic, cultural and ideological difference, toward liberation from capital, colonialism and the state. A former college newspaper astrologer with a multimedia journalism background, Kathryn is invested in growing access to art and information through audio and visual design. Topics she explores in any and every context include Taylor Swift, anarchism, and folk Catholicism.

Available for freelance projects and collaborations in script consultation, production dramaturgy, audio, copywriting & editing, and print & front-end design.
