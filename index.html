Bio / Contact Astrology

Kathryn de la Rosa is a writer and freelance dramaturg and audio producer. Between mediums, they collaborate with playwrights, theaters and brands to tell stories via script development, production dramaturgy, and radio narrative both reported and fictional. They write essays and plays about middle America, leftist politics, Catholicism, Filipino anti-imperialism, and where they all meet.

Friends, strangers and enemies know them as a Swiftie, and they're a practicing astrologer and theological dilettante. They hold degrees in theatre & drama and journalism from Indiana University, and did most of their growing up in Kentucky and Arkansas.
