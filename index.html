Kathryn de la Rosa Services Astrology

Kathryn de la Rosa is a writer and freelance dramaturg and audio producer. Between mediums, they collaborate with playwrights, theaters and brands to tell stories via script development, production dramaturgy, and radio narrative both reported and fictional. They write essays and plays about rurality, leftist politics, Catholicism, Filipino anti-imperialism, and where they all meet. They aspire to theater that is buoyantly radical, with a deep love for people, their gods and their communities in the middle of America and the edges of its empire.

Friends, strangers and enemies know them as a Swiftie, and they're a practicing astrologer and theological dilettante. They hold degrees in theatre & drama and journalism from Indiana University, and did most of their growing up in Kentucky and Arkansas.
