about

I live and create at the intersection of diaspora and provinciality. I’m drawn to work that engages a diversity of place, race, and ideas, toward liberation from capital, colonialism and the state. I write plays set in Kentucky, Indiana and the Philippines, with love and complication. I believe in regional theater, and support and develop new plays by QPOC and rural writers.
