Kathryn de la Rosa is a playwright, dramaturg and radio producer based in Louisville, writing and working at the intersection of diaspora and provinciality. Their plays engage a diversity of place, race and ideology, toward liberation from capital, colonialism and the state. A media jack-of-all-trades, Kathryn is interested in growing access to art and information through audio and visual design. Topics she explores in any context include astrology, anarchism, Taylor Swift, and folk Catholicism.

Available for freelance projects and collaborations in production dramaturgy, script consultation, audio production, copywriting & editing, front-end web design, and social curation starting: July 20.
