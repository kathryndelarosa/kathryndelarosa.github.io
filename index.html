Kathryn de la Rosa is a dramaturg, playwright and audio producer from Kentucky.

Across mediums, they collaborate with writers, theaters and brands to tell stories via script development, production dramaturgy, and radio narrative both reported and fictional. They aspire to theater that is buoyantly radical, with love for people, their gods and their communities in the middle of America and the edges of its empire.

Friends and strangers know them as a Swiftie, and for fun they knit, play guitar and code websites. They hold degrees in theatre & drama and journalism from Indiana University, where they wrote the Indiana Daily Student astrology column. They plan to start graduate studies in theology in fall 2021.

Kathryn is currently open to dramaturgy and audio editing projects.
