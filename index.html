Kathryn de la Rosa is a religion graduate student whose professional career has mostly been in theater as a dramaturg and producer of new plays, most recently the Audible Emerging Playwright commissions Dirty Laundry by Mathilde Dratwa and Vanessa in Bed by Diana Grisanti.

Their research and creative projects explore Christianity in southeast and east Asia, especially early modern Jesuit and Dominican missions. Their MA thesis is about the canonization of Lorenzo Ruiz, toward a larger work on martyrdom and nationalism.

They have also studied western astrology for seven years, lately with the Fagan-Bradley ayanamsa.
