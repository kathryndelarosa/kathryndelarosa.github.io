Hi there! I'm Kathryn de la Rosa ♓, a Filipinx-Kentuckian writer, dramaturg & audio producer.

Across mediums, my work ☀️ is the same: collaborating with playwrights, theaters and brands to tell stories, via new play & production dramaturgy and narrative podcasts 🎧.

My plays 🖋️ are about postcolonialism, diaspora, rurality, and belief, with humor and love for young womxn in the middle of America. I write a newsletter about, so far, Taylor Swift 🐍, theater and Christian communism.

In past lives, I've been a college newspaper astrologer 🔮 and a violin repairman 🎻 . My main hobbies are GitHub and guitar.
