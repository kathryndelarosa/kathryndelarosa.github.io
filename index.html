Kathryn de la Rosa (she/siya) is a dramaturg and producer from Kentucky primarily working on new plays. Her most recent work has been with Audible Theatre's Emerging Playwright Commissions as associate producer. She has also written plays of her own about Filipinos in the middle of America and does weird weekend side projects like building a Nick Cave lyric search app.
