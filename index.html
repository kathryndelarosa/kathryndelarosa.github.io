Oh hey! It's me, Kathryn de la Rosa.

I’m a genderqueer Pilipina dramaturg & playwright born and bred in Kentucky, currently an apprentice at Actors Theatre of Louisville.

I like working on new plays, adaptations, and verbatim and verse drama. I write plays about identity and place, resistance and healing, spirituality and magic — the contradictions and conjunctions that disrupt power, binaries and division.

I’m a jack-of-all-trades (emphasis on master of none): I coded this website with my bare hands, lived past lives as radio producer and sound designer, and seriously study and practice astrology.

I graduated from Indiana University with a BA in theater and journalism. I’m available for writing, production dramaturgy, script consultation, audio and digital projects, and am always down to talk Taylor Swift.
