Kathryn de la Rosa (b. 1997, Prestonsburg, Kentucky) is a dramaturg, sound designer, writer, radio producer, front-end web developer, and occasional actor/cellist. She is a senior in her third and final season with IU Theatre. Her research and artistic interests lie in rural, queer and post-colonial studies and intersections of performance and mass media. She's capping her dual degree with a thesis on the ethics and practices of documentary theatermaking.

In her life as a multimedia journalist, she executive produced American Student Radio, assistant produced The Quietus Hour, and contributed to programs at WFHB. She is also a print and web designer willing and able to use UX to support dramaturgy.

Recent and forthcoming projects include:
Vinegar Tom
Titus Andronicus
High School Coven
Barbecue
10 Out of 12
The Resistible Rise of Arturo Ui
The Exonerated

Beyond theater, she's into tarot, astrology, and Paddington Bear. Her truest joy is baking cornbread.
