Kathryn de la Rosa is a Filipino-Kentuckian scholar of religion researching Catholicism in east and insular Asia. She is currently writing an MA thesis about the canonization of Lorenzo Ruiz, toward a larger project on the politics of martyrdom. Most of their professional work has been in theater as a new play dramaturg and producer.
