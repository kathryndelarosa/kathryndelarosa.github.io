Kathryn de la Rosa is a dramaturg, playwright and audio producer.

Between mediums, they collaborate with writers, theaters and brands to tell stories via script development, production dramaturgy, and radio narrative both reported and fictional. They aspire to theater that is buoyantly radical, with love for people, their gods and their communities in the middle of America and the edges of its empire.

Friends and strangers know them as a Swiftie, and they're a practicing astrologer, theological dilettante, and knitter. They hold degrees in theatre & drama and journalism from Indiana University, and did most of their growing up in Kentucky and Arkansas.

Kathryn is currently open to dramaturgy and audio editing projects, and waffling on about grad school.
