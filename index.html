Kathryn de la Rosa (she/her)

I’m a Pisces 🐠 and a dramaturg, writer & audio producer. I like to work on new plays, adaptations, and stories that engage anti-urbanism. Kentucky born and bred 🥃, I’m in the 2019-20 training company at Actors Theatre of Louisville.

Favorite projects: Little Women, Vinegar Tom 🐈, Where We Stand — The New Harmony Project, Haus of Mirth 🍎, The Resistible Rise of Arturo Ui, High School Coven 🐍, 10 Out of 12.

This fall, I workshop my play Ribs 🥂 with University Players.

I’m available for production dramaturgy, script consultation, writing, and anything audio.
