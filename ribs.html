Ribs

4w, full-length comedy

“Did you guys know spinster age is 22?” 21-year-old Liha asks her 21-year-old roommates. Four girls grow up in their last four months of undergrad: Margo, an ex-College Republican; would-be witch Katie; Josie, hardened and wry; and meme-illiterate Liha. A sort-of sex comedy in an accidental virgin vault, for women who go to IU Bloomington. The Bachelor, Carmelite nuns, and the natyashastra coalesce into a messy, braless portrait of modern womanhood.

University Players, workshop dir. Hannah Keeler, Sept. 2019
IU Bloomington, staged reading dir. Eleanor Sobczyk, May 2019
