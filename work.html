Resume and curriculum vitae.

Highlights below.

Actors Theatre of Louisville

Kathryn was a 2019/20 dramaturgy & literary management apprentice. They assisted with dramaturgy for Are You There?, Where the Mountain Meets the Sea (Jeff Augustin profile) and FLEX in the 44th Humana Festival, as well as The Wolves. They served as dramaturg on Louisville // Kentucky // USA and the Solo Mio series in the Professional Training Company season of new works.

After the premature end of their apprenticeship due to COVID-19, Kathryn collaborated with Actors Theatre on digital content & engagement projects for Actors Theatre Direct.

The New Harmony Project

Kathryn was a literary assistant at the 2019 Spring Conference, primarily working on Where We Stand by Donnetta Lavinia Grays. With resident dramaturg Phaedra Scott, they also lent dramaturgical support to writers-in-residence Jennifer Barclay and Robert Schenkkan.

Indiana Daily Student

On a Christmas break whim, Kathryn pitched what would become an immensely popular, deeply polarizing weekly astrology column, breaking an Indiana Daily Student pageview record with their March 2019 Mercury retrograde column.

IU Theatre & Summer Theatre

Kathryn dramaturged Little Women for IU Summer Theatre, after three years as a dramaturg on mainstage academic productions, including the world premiere of Haus of Mirth in the At First Sight New Play Festival. They dramaturged Vinegar Tom, The Resistible Rise of Arturo Ui and The Exonerated and variously sound designed, stage managed and performed in student productions and staged readings.
