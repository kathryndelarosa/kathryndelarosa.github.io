Tarot + astrology

I distrust the card reader or astrologer who hides in mystique. Like dramaturgy, my readings are generative and questioning. I prioritize understanding and illustrate cards and concepts with pop culture and humor. If you want a taste for my approach to all things woo, check out my newsletter archive. I see divination as a tool for advice and self-reflection, not fortune-telling.
