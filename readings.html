Tarot + astrology

I distrust the card reader or astrologer who hides in mystique. Like dramaturgy, my readings are generative and questioning. I prioritize understanding and illustrate cards and concepts with pop culture and humor. I don't hoard answers like a psychic gatekeeper, but hope you come away seeing the occult as accessible and applicable in your life.

I see divination as a tool for advice and reflection, not fortune-telling. My fees reflect my experience. If you hesitate to take a chance on someone who's still learning, I'm happy to redirect you to seasoned readers I trust.

Book a reading or find out more at kathrynrdelarosa@gmail.com. I take PayPal and Venmo, but if price is a barrier, I'm pro-bartering. Just ask!
