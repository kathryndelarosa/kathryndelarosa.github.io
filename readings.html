Tarot + astrology

Readers of my tarot newsletter or astrology column already know my practice is inextricable from theater. I'm here to serve all and everyone, but I adore reading for creatives and QWOC.

A reading with me is generative and questioning. Think of it as collaboration rather than consultation. I prioritize access over mystique, using pop culture and humor to help you understand yourself and your path. If you're after predictions of your future, velvet drapes and a fog machine, look elsewhere.

Schedule a reading session, order an email reading, or find out more at kathryndelarosa@icloud.com. I take PayPal and Venmo, but if price is a barrier, I'm pro-bartering. Just ask!
