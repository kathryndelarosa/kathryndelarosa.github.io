Current projects

At Actors Theatre, I split my time between the mainstage and Professional Training Company seasons. In the 2020 Humana Festival, I'm assisting with dramaturgy for Jeff Augustin’s Where the Mountain Meets the Sea, Candrice Jones’ FLEX, and the PTC commission Are You There? by Vivian Barnes, Jonathan Norton and Gab Reisman. Earlier, I assisted on The Wolves. I served as dramaturg for half of the Solo Mio series written and performed by acting apprentices, which played in three rounds in September and October, and the PTC New Play Projects commission Louisville // Kentucky // USA by Lisa Sanaye Dring.

I spent the first two weeks of December workshopping my play Ribs with University Players, which culminated in a weekend of public readings at Indiana University.

After graduating in May 2019, I was a literary assistant at The New Harmony Project, where I worked on Donnetta Lavinia Grays’ Where We Stand and lent dramaturgical support to Jennifer Barclay and Robert Schenkkan. I stuck around in Bloomington and got my first professional dramaturgy credit under my belt with IU Summer Theatre’s Little Women.
