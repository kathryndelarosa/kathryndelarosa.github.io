Kathryn de la Rosa ♃ Services Astrology

I've studied Western astrology seriously since 2017, and I wrote and illustrated the Indiana Daily Student's weekly astrology column with a fraction of the knowledge I have now. I started doing more formal readings for friends in 2020, due in no small part to a lack of parties where I'd squint at Astrodienst on my phone and yell about people's Saturns.

I understand astrology as a man-made system like language or religion, created in the context of a certain time to serve the interests of a certain class—in short, materially, and not dissimilar to how I think of Christianity. Astrology has history and lineage to learn before you break and remake it into something new. I start with whole sign houses and traditional Hellenistic rulerships, and bring in outer planets and other modern concepts as needed. I'm currently studying evolutionary astrology, particularly Pluto and the lunar nodes.
