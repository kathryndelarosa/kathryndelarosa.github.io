Kathryn de la Rosa Services Astrology

I've studied western astrology seriously since 2017, and I wrote and illustrated the Indiana Daily Student's weekly astrology column with a fraction of the knowledge I have now. I started doing more formal readings for friends in 2020, due in no small part to a lack of parties where I'd squint at Astrodienst on my phone and yell about people's Saturns.

I neither own crystals or think my tarot decks have feelings. I understand astrology as a man-made system like language or religion, created in the context of a certain time to serve the interests of a certain class. It’s not dissimilar to how I think of Christianity: a tradition with a (less violent) history to study before you break and remake it into something new. I start with whole sign houses and traditional rulerships, and introduce the outer planets and other modern concepts as needed.
