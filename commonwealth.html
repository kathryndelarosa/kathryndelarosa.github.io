Common Wealth

8w/3m, full-length

For 26 years, the annual Cardinal Summer Arts Intensive has chosen gifted high schoolers from seven states to participate in a prestigious 3-week program. In the staff office, where college-age camp counselors work and unwind, a racist incident in the student body threatens the tacit inequities of liberalism.

What is a safe space? Who consructs them, and at whose cost? A study of tokenism, call-out culture, virtue signaling, and the nonprofit sector.
