Kathryn de la Rosa (they/she) is a Filipinx writer, dramaturg, audio producer and former college newspaper astrologer from Kentucky. Between mediums, their work is the same: collaborating with writers, theaters and brands to tell stories. They bring the same ear for character and dramatic structure to live theater, audio drama, and radio narrative.

They have worked as a dramaturg at Actors Theatre of Louisville, The New Harmony Project, Working Title Playwrights, College of the Holy Cross, and Indiana University, and produced and edited podcasts for Larj Media, Playing On Air, and Actors Theatre of Louisville. They did most of their growing up in Kentucky and Arkansas, and graduated from Indiana University with degrees in theater and journalism.
