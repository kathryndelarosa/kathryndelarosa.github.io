About

Kathryn writes plays in the meeting of provincialism and diaspora, about radical, mystical, chaotic womxn in the Midwest and the Philippines. Her plays have been developed with her Actors Theatre of Louisville apprentice cohort, in IU Theatre's At First Sight Festival, and by University Players.

They wrote a polarizing astrology column for the Indiana Daily Student, and has had poems published in Canvas magazine.

Kathryn was a 2019–20 dramaturgy apprentice at Actors Theatre of Louisville and previously interned with The New Harmony Project.

Thay have produced audio stories with American Student Radio and branded podcasts for Indiana University and Actors Theatre.
