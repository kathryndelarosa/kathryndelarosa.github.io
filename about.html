About

Kathryn works in the meeting of provincialism and diaspora. They write plays about radical, mystical, chaotic womxn in the middle of America. Their work soaks in contradictions: identity and place, magic and belief, healing from history, tradition and liberation.

Kathryn's plays have been developed at Indiana University and with the Professional Training Company at Actors Theatre of Louisville, where she was a 2019–20 dramaturgy apprentice.

Kathryn contributes to Queer Kentucky and wrote a polarizing astrology column for the Indiana Daily Student. They previously interned at The New Harmony Project.

BA & BAJ Indiana University.
