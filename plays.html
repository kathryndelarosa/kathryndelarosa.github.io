Ribs

4f — “Did you know spinster age is 22?” 21-year-old Liha asks her 21-year-old roommates. A sort-of sex comedy in an accidental virgin vault, Ribs explores femininity, sexuality, and privilege from the strange, funny, vulgar confines of a Bloomington student rental. Between The Bachelor and graduation, Title IX and astrology, Margo, Liha, Josie, and Katie learn to feel and fight at the end of a decade in middle America.

2f, 2m, 1 crocodile — A Filipino crocodile hunter dies, his American granddaughter comes home, and a tourist economy explodes around a 20-foot long suspected killer named in his honor. The line between dead man and dying reptile blurs as Aimee, her aunt, and their community untangle the political, historical and divine in keeping the world's largest crocodile in captivity.

7f, 3m — A racist incident at an arts summer camp forces eight college-aged counselors to do damage control. What is a safe space? Who constructs them, and at whose cost? On liberalism, fragility, progress, and TikTok teens.
