laughing till our ribs get tough

Ribs explores femininity, sexuality, and class privilege in the familiar confines of a student rental in a Midwestern college town. Between laughter and horror, The Bachelor and Title IX, roommates Margo, Liha, Josie, and Katie find their footing in — and against — the political spectrum, the gender binary, and being young in America.

University Players, workshop dir. Hannah Keeler, Sept. 2019
IU Bloomington, staged reading dir. Eleanor Sobczyk, May 2019

Common wealth

Each year, the Cardinal Summer Arts Intensive invites 100 gifted high schoolers from seven states to participate in a prestigious 3-week training program. In the staff office, where college-age camp counselors work and unwind, a racist incident in the student body tests the tacit inequities that keep liberalism afloat.

What is a safe space? Who consructs them, and at whose cost? A study of tokenism, call-out culture, virtue signaling, and the nonprofit sector.
