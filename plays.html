Ribs

“Did you know spinster age is 22?” 21-year-old Liha asks her 21-year-old roommates. A sort-of sex comedy in an accidental virgin vault, Ribs explores femininity, sexuality, and privilege from the strange, funny, vulgar confines of a Bloomington student rental. Between The Bachelor and graduation, Title IX and astrology, Margo, Liha, Josie, and Katie learn to feel — and fight — at the end of a decade in middle America.

Crocodile Play (for Lolong)

An imagining of the death and lives of the once-largest crocodile in activity. When crocodile hunter Lolong's granddaugther comes from America for his funeral, she finds a 20-foot saltwater crocodile named in his honor. The line between dead man and dying reptile blurs as Aimee, her aunt, and their southern Filipino community untangle the political, historical and divine meaning in Lolong’s capture.
