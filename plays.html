Holy Virgins

full-length, 2 filipinx + 2 white women

Western Kentucky, 2012. Sam, Marina and Jesse are about to be confirmed Catholics, licensed drivers and suspended from Immaculate Conception High School. A sour-sweet dark comedy about queer girlhood, forgiving your mom, and what to believe.

Joy & Joyce

full-length, 2 Filipinas, 2 Chinese women, 1 Filipino, 3 Chinese men

Joyce Chen starts a restaurant chain and cookware empire in midcentury Massachusetts, and 70 years later in Indiana, Joy Cunanan's wannabe food vlogger, Yang Gang boyfriend is getting a wok to the head. On boba liberalism, labor export, pan-Asian solidarity, pots and pans, and U.S. and Chinese imperialism in the west Philippine sea.

Pasyon Play

one-act, 8 filipinos + 3 filipinas

In the aftermath of the First Quarter Storm, a Kapampangan family stages Christ's death and resurrection. After Sarah Ruhl, with debts to Joma Sisson, Sir Anril Tiatco and Eleazar S. Fernandez.

The Best Day

10–15 minutes, 2 filipinas + 1 white woman

A Filipino mom + her daughter's college orientation + the 1989 World tour = an unwelcome, expensive surprise.
