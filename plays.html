Ribs

“Did you know spinster age is 22?” 21-year-old Liha asks her 21-year-old roommates. A sort-of sex comedy in an accidental virgin vault, Ribs explores femininity, sexuality, and privilege from the strange, funny, vulgar confines of a Bloomington student rental. Between The Bachelor and graduation, Title IX and astrology, Margo, Liha, Josie, and Katie learn to feel — and fight — in the middle of America.

University Players, workshop dir. Hannah Keeler, Sept. 2019
IU Bloomington, staged reading dir. Eleanor Sobczyk, May 2019

Common wealth

Each year, the Cardinal Summer Arts Intensive invites 100 gifted high schoolers from seven states to participate in a prestigious 3-week training program. In the staff office, where college-age camp counselors work and unwind, a racist incident in the student body tests the tacit inequities that keep liberalism afloat. What is a safe space? Who consructs them, and at whose cost? A study of tokenism, call-out culture, virtue signaling, and the nonprofit sector.
