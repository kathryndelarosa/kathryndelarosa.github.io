Hello! I'm Kathryn, a queer Pilipina playwright,
dramaturg & digital creator from Kentucky.

I live and create at the intersection of diaspora and provinciality.

I’m drawn to work that engages a diversity of place, race, and ideas,
toward liberation from capital and empire;
I write plays set in Kentucky, Indiana and the Philippines, with love and complication;
I believe regional theater is meant to elevate queer, immigrant and rural voices.

I also produce podcasts and code websites.

19/20 Actors Theatre of Louisville PTC.
BA Theatre & Drama/BAJ Journalism Indiana University.

Available for freelance projects and collaborations in:
production dramaturgy, script reading & consultation, audio production,
copywriting & editing, front-end web design and social curation.

Based in Louisville, Kentucky.
